The Royal Mummies Parade

On April 3rd, 2021, Egypt held a grand event called the Royal Mummies Parade.
It was a historic moment that amazed millions around the world.
Twenty-two mummies of ancient Egyptian kings and queens were moved with great honor.
The parade started at the Egyptian Museum in Tahrir Square.
The mummies were transferred to the National Museum of Egyptian Civilization in Fustat.
Each mummy was placed in a golden vehicle designed like a pharaonic coffin.
The parade included music, lights, and ancient Egyptian style performances.
It showed the world the greatness of Egyptâ€™s history and culture.
Millions watched the event live on TV and online.
This parade became a symbol of pride for all Egyptians.
